How The Internet Changed Ecucation Forever

Throughout time, the educational system has remained scarely similar. Whilst the standard of education has risen, the bar the students are expected to achieve has sky rocketed to beyond anything that is even theasable. Before the internet was created the main source of information was from books and newspapers and so everyone heavily relied on the information that they had read before. This was very limiting because it is just not possible to gather all the information that todays students need in books, students of today need a lot more assistance because they have a lot more to take in. It almost isn't fair. Geoff Barton, general secretary of the heads' union ASCL, recently stated: "While the increased rigour may have been in the interest of the more able students, it had come at the expense of the more vulnerable ones." He then called for the exams to be tweaked to make them "less of an ordeal" and said a more "humane way" to assess the abilities of young people was required."
